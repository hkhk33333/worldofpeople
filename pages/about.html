World of People

Connecting humanity through personal stories

About the Project

"World of People" is a global initiative that aims to highlight our shared human experience through authentic conversations with people from all walks of life and corners of the globe.

Our Mission

To foster empathy and connection by revealing how, despite our different backgrounds, we all face similar struggles, joys, and questions in life.

How It Works

We approach strangers in different countries and ask them personal questions about their lives, challenges, hopes, and perspectives. These candid moments are captured on video to create a global tapestry of human experience.

Join the Movement

Soon, you'll be able to participate by conducting your own interviews or sharing your own stories. Together, we can build a platform that celebrates our common humanity.
